About Healthy Living Blog

Welcome! I’m Christine Nyawara, a wellness enthusiast, certified nutrition coach, and passionate writer.
On this blog, I share tips, ideas, and personal stories around healthy eating, daily exercise, mindfulness, and ways to cultivate a balanced life.


My mission is to help you live healthier, happier, and more mindful lives—without extremes.
Whether you're just starting out or continuing your wellness journey, I hope here you find inspiration, guidance, and community.


When I'm not writing or coaching, you can find me hiking local trails, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or curled up with a good book. I believe wellness is a lifelong journey, and I'm excited to share mine with you.
